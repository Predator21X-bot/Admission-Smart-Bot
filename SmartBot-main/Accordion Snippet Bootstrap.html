Frequently Asked Questions

ABOUT UNIVERSITY

From where can I get the information about the University?
Please explore IGDTUW Website and/or read IGDTUW Admission Brochure 2021-22.
Is the hostel facility available?
Yes, but limited hostel facility is available in IGDTUW (refer Admission Brochure)
Is the mess facility available?
Yes, a mess facility is available in IGDTUW.
Is the transport facility (college bus) available?
No but IGDTUW is well connected to the metro and other public transport.
What is the placement record of last year?
Please explore IGDTUW Website and/or read IGDTUW Admission Brochure 2021-22.
What is the medium of instruction?
English.
Do I have to stay on campus?
It’s not necessary for a student to stay at hostel although we have hostels which are fully furnished , Wi-Fi connectivity, 24*7 power supplies and a very high degree of hygiene maintained therein.
Does the campus have a library?
Yes, each faculty of IGDTUW has a very rich library. It has a very well-equipped, spacious and computerized library with a vast collection of books from national and international publishers, magazines, journals, statistical publications and a rich collection of audio-video cassettes and CDs for various subjects.
How active is the Training and Placements Department of IGDTUW?
We have an active and centralized Training and Placement Cell (TPC) that works throughout the year with commendable efficacy and effectiveness. It organizes training for students in order to enhance their personality, team spirit, systematic planning and so on.
Is there a medical facility available on campus?
Yes, we have a well-equipped medical care unit and an isolation ward under the care and supervision of a Resident Lady Doctor. We also have full-time qualified female nursing staff to assist the doctor. Routine medical checkup by specialist medical practitioners are also provided at regular intervals to maintain a healthy and hygienic atmosphere at IGDTUW.
What co-curricular activities are conducted on the campus?
Here at IGDTUW, we emphasize upon and work for an all-round development of our students for which various co-curricular activities like sports (which include both indoor and outdoor games), cultural meets, fests and competitions are held from time to time.
What about the safety of students on campus?
We, at IGDTUW, pay special attention to students’ safety and security. The security of students is taken care of by Retired military personnel who provide round the clock security within the campus. On regular tours and excursions, the students are accompanied by faculty members and staff for their safety and well being.
How can I communicate with the university or my ward?
We have telephone extensions and various help-lines which are available 24*7 for the convenience of the students as well as parents.

ADMISSION RELATED QUERIES

What are the eligibility criteria for various courses?
Admission to BTech and BArch is subject to a valid score in JEE Main followed by Joint Admission Counselling (JAC). BBA admissions are merit-based. Under the PG domain, programs like M.Tech with 5 specializations, MBA, MCA, and M.Plan. are offered. For admission to the M.Tech. and M.Plan. programs, a valid score in GATE is mandatory. Admission to MCA is conducted through an entrance test conducted by IGDTUW while admission to the MBA will be strictly based on the candidate’s performance in CAT followed by GD and PI. For Ph.D, IGDTUW conducts Research Aptitude Test (RAT) followed by an interview. CSIR-NET/ UGC-NET/ GATE or any other equivalent exam qualified candidates are exempted from RAT. To apply for admission, the candidate’s must visit the official website of the institute. Please refer to IGDTUW Admission Brochure 2021-22, for eligibility criteria. Note: Only female candidates are eligible.
What is the last date for withdrawal of admission?
Please refer to the Admission Brochure for withdrawal dates or keep a track of dates from the official website.
How do I apply for the programme (Step by Step Guide to fill IGDTUW Admission Application Form) ?
There are 5 simple steps to be followed to apply online: Step 1: Registration ● Click on the direct link provided on this page. ● Next, click on the button that says “New User Register Here”. ● A registration form is going to pop up. Fill in all the details and set up a password. Step 2: Complete Application Form After registering successfully, fill the rest of the details in the IGDTUW 2020 Application Form. Candidates need to provide following details while applying: ● Personal Information ● Course, one wishes to apply for (if applying for more than 1 course, a separate application form and fee is to be submitted for each course) ● Academic details ● Work Experience Step 3: Upload Documents In this step, candidates have to upload the required certificate/documents in prescribed format and size. Step 4: Pay Application Fee After completing all the above steps, proceed to pay the fee online. Applications without fee payment are rejected. Step 5: Print Application Form Finally, after payment of fee and submission of form, take a print-out of IGDTUW Application Form 2021 for future references. There is no need to send a hard copy of the form to the University.

What are the specializations offered in B.Tech at IGDTUW?
Following are the specializations offered in B.Tech at IGDTUW:
  • Computer science and Artificial Intelligence (CSAI)
  • Computer science
  • Information Technology
  • Mechanical & Automation
  • Electronics & Communication
What is the IGDTUW B.Tech and B.Arch Eligibility Criteria ?
For the B.Tech program, the basic eligibility for is 60% marks in 10+2 in the Science group.
For the B.Arch program, the basic eligibility is 50% marks in 10+2 with Mathematics.
